PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 15, 2026

The short version: we store your Reddit username, an account identifier, and your golf scores so the daily leaderboard works. No emails, no ads, no tracking pixels, no selling anything to anyone. That's the whole game.

1. WHO WE ARE

Hellish Golf ("the Game", "we", "us") is a daily golf game played inside the Reddit feed, built on Reddit's Developer Platform (Devvit) by an independent developer. It is not operated by Reddit, Inc. Reddit's own handling of your account is covered by the Reddit Privacy Policy.

2. WHAT WE COLLECT

When you play, our servers store:

DataWhy
Reddit username & Reddit account ID To show your name on the daily leaderboard and keep one score per player.
Scores: strokes, completion time, daily streak To rank the leaderboard and track your streak.
Replay moves (the shots you took on a hole) To let other players watch run replays and to verify scores.
Map ID, date, and the Reddit post you played on To attach scores to the correct daily challenge.
Timestamps (first seen / last seen) Basic housekeeping and anti-cheat.

We do not collect email addresses, real names, passwords, payment details, precise location, or device advertising identifiers. We do not use analytics trackers, advertising SDKs, or cookies of our own.

3. WHERE YOUR DATA LIVES

  • Game data is processed by Reddit's Devvit platform and stored in a database hosted by Supabase, reached through our server at hellishgolf.xyz (hosted on Vercel).
  • Database writes happen server-side only; your browser never gets write access.
  • Hosting providers may log standard technical data (like IP addresses) as part of operating their infrastructure, per their own policies.

4. WHO CAN SEE YOUR DATA

  • Other players can see your Reddit username, score, streak, and replays on the leaderboard — the same username that is already public on Reddit.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers or data brokers. Ever.
  • We may disclose data if required by law.

5. HOW LONG WE KEEP IT

Scores and replays are kept so historical daily leaderboards keep working. If you want your data removed, just ask (see Contact below) and we will delete your leaderboard entries, replays, and player record.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on where you live (e.g., GDPR in the EU/UK, CCPA in California), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, or object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us — we'll handle it. Since we only store your public Reddit identity and game scores, requests are usually simple.

7. CHILDREN

The Game is played through Reddit and is intended for users who meet Reddit's minimum age requirements (13+, or older where local law requires). We do not knowingly collect data from children below those ages.

8. SECURITY

All traffic uses HTTPS. Database access is restricted to our server with row-level security enabled; public and anonymous roles have no access to stored tables.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be noted in the Game or on r/hellishgolf_dev.

10. CONTACT

For privacy questions or deletion requests, message the developer on Reddit via r/hellishgolf_dev.
